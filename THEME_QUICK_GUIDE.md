# Theme Switching Quick Guide

## How to Change Your Theme

1. **Locate the Theme Selector**
   - Look for the palette icon (ðŸŽ¨) in the top navigation bar
   - It's located next to your user profile icon

2. **Open the Theme Menu**
   - Click on the palette icon
   - A dropdown menu will appear showing three theme options

3. **Choose Your Theme**
   - **Star Admin 2** - Modern Bootstrap with customizable colors
   - **Soft UI Dashboard** - Premium design with soft shadows and gradients
   - **Sneat Admin** - Clean and professional Bootstrap 5 interface

4. **Apply the Theme**
   - Click on your preferred theme
   - The page will reload automatically with your new theme
   - Your choice is saved and will persist across sessions
